Tuna Sandwich

Description

This is a recipe guide to making a Tuna Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Bread
  • Mayo
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Take tuna out of tuna can and put into a bowl
  2. Cut up the avocado and put it into the same bowl
  3. Take a spoonful of mayo and put into the bowl
  4. Take a fork/spoon and mix up the items in the bowl together
  5. Using a spoon scoop the tuna mix from the bowl and spread onto bread (you can toast or not)
  6. Put the other bread side on and enjoy!
